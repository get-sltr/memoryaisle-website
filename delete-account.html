Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go

This action is permanent

Account deletion cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed.

What happens when you delete your account

When you delete your MemoryAisle account, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Personal Information: Your email address, name, and profile photo
  • Shopping Lists: All lists you've created and their items
  • Meal Plans: All saved meal plans and recipes
  • Traditions: Your custom traditions and holiday settings
  • Preferences: All app preferences and settings
  • Receipt Data: Scanned receipts and spending history
  • Family Connections: You will be removed from all family groups

What is NOT deleted

  • Shared Lists: Lists shared with family members will remain accessible to them (your contributions will show as "Deleted User")
  • Anonymized Analytics: Aggregated, non-identifiable usage data may be retained
  • Legal Records: Transaction records required for legal/financial compliance

Before you delete

Please consider the following:

  • Export your data: You can export your shopping lists and recipes from the app before deleting
  • Cancel subscription: If you have a Premium subscription, cancel it first to avoid future charges
  • Notify family: Let family members know if you're leaving shared lists

How to delete your account

Method 1: In the App (Recommended)

  1. Open MemoryAisle
  2. Go to Settings (tap your profile icon)
  3. Scroll down to Account
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm your decision
  6. Your account will be deleted immediately

Method 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by email:

  1. Send an email to delete@memoryaisle.app
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the email address associated with your account
  4. We will verify your identity and process the request within 7 days

Data deletion timeline

Immediate Account access revoked, login disabled
Within 24 hours Personal data removed from active systems
Within 30 days Data removed from all backup systems
Within 90 days Complete purge from cold storage backups
