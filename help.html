Everything you need to know about MemoryAisle

Welcome to the MemoryAisle Help Center. Find answers to common questions, learn how to use features, and get the most out of your smart grocery shopping assistant.

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Download MemoryAisle from the App Store, open the app, and sign up using your email, Apple ID, Google account, or Facebook. Your account syncs across all your devices automatically.

How do I create my first shopping list?

After signing in, tap the "+" button on the home screen or simply ask Mira "Create a new shopping list for [store name]." You can add items by typing, using voice commands, or asking Mira for suggestions.

What is Mira?

Mira is your AI-powered shopping assistant. She can help you create lists, suggest recipes, generate meal plans, find deals, and answer questions about groceries. Tap the Mira icon or use voice commands to interact with her.

Features

Shopping Lists

  • Create lists: Tap "+" or ask Mira to create a list for a specific store
  • Add items: Type, speak, or ask Mira for suggestions
  • Check off items: Tap an item to mark it as complete
  • Organize: Items are automatically categorized by aisle
  • Share: Invite family members to collaborate on lists in real-time

Mira AI Assistant

  • Voice commands: "Add milk and eggs to my Costco list"
  • Recipe help: "What do I need for chicken alfredo?"
  • Meal planning: "Create a meal plan for the week"
  • Smart suggestions: "What's on sale at Trader Joe's?"

Family Sharing

  • Invite family members via email or phone number
  • Real-time sync across all family devices
  • See who added what and when items are checked off
  • Set dietary preferences and allergies for the whole family

Meal Plans & Recipes

  • Generate personalized meal plans based on your preferences
  • Get recipes that account for dietary restrictions
  • Automatically add recipe ingredients to your shopping list
  • Save favorite recipes for later

Store Geofencing

  • Get notified when you arrive at a saved store location
  • Your shopping list opens automatically
  • Enable in Settings > Notifications > Location Reminders

Receipt Scanning (Premium)

  • Scan receipts to track spending
  • Automatically match items to your lists
  • Build purchase history for better suggestions

How much does Premium cost?

Subscription is auto-renewable and can be canceled anytime through your device settings.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime through your device's subscription settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > MemoryAisle > Cancel
  • Android: Play Store > Profile > Payments & subscriptions > Subscriptions > MemoryAisle > Cancel

Your Premium features will remain active until the end of your billing period.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple (iOS) or Google (Android). Contact their support for refund requests. We cannot process refunds directly.

Account & Privacy

How do I change my email address?

Currently, email changes require contacting support. Email us at support@memoryaisle.app from your registered email address.

How do I delete my account?

Visit the Delete Account page or go to Settings > Account > Delete Account in the app. Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption, secure authentication, and row-level security. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

Does Mira store my conversations?

Mira conversations are processed to provide responses but are not stored long-term or used for AI training. Only your shopping lists and preferences are saved.

Troubleshooting

The app won't sync

  • Check your internet connection
  • Pull down on the main screen to force refresh
  • Sign out and sign back in
  • Delete and reinstall the app (your data is backed up)

Mira isn't responding

  • Check your internet connection
  • If you're on the free plan, you may have reached your daily limit
  • Try closing and reopening the app

Voice commands aren't working

  • Ensure microphone permissions are enabled in your device settings
  • Speak clearly after tapping the microphone icon
  • Check that your device isn't on silent/mute

Notifications aren't appearing

  • Enable notifications in your device settings for MemoryAisle
  • Check that Do Not Disturb is turned off
  • For location-based notifications, ensure location permissions are set to "Always"
