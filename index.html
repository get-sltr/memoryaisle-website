I · Features

Six tools that move with you, not at you.

I

Protein-First Meal Plans

Mira generates daily meals optimized for your protein target and dietary needs. Full cookbook-style recipes with exact ingredients and step-by-step instructions.

II

Body Composition Tracking

Track weight, lean mass, and body fat over time. See your muscle preservation score. Share weekly reports with your doctor.

III

Smart Grocery Lists

Scan barcodes in-store and get instant nutrition verdicts. Your grocery list is auto-generated from your meal plan.

IV

Training-Day Nutrition

Pre-workout carbs, post-workout protein, rest-day adjustments. The app adapts to whether you trained today.

V

GLP-1 Medication Support

On a GLP-1? The app adapts to your injection cycle or oral dosing schedule. Nausea-safe meals on tough days, bigger portions when appetite returns.

VI

Mira, Your AI Companion

Talk to Mira by voice or text. She knows your protein deficit, training schedule, and goals. She adapts in real time.

II · Privacy

Your health data is sacred.

I

Medication stays on your phone

Our AI only sees anonymous categories like "GLP-1, low dose, day 2." Never your prescription or brand name.

II

Voice is never recorded

Speech recognition runs on-device using Apple's technology. Only the text reaches Mira. Audio is discarded instantly.

III

Encrypted twice

Your health data is protected by two independent layers of encryption. One at the infrastructure level, one at the application level. Neither can be bypassed without the other.

IV

Delete means delete

One tap to erase everything. All data permanently removed within 30 days. No questions asked.
