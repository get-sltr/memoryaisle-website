MemoryAisle launches to help GLP-1 users lose fat, not muscle.

A body-composition-first nutrition app that adapts to your appetite, symptoms, and dose cycle — now on the App Store.

Los Angeles, CA · June 3, 2026

Today MemoryAisle launches on the App Store with a simple, contrarian premise: on a GLP-1 medication, how much you lose matters far less than what you lose. The app is built to help people on Ozempic, Wegovy, Zepbound, and Mounjaro — and anyone in a calorie deficit — lose fat while protecting the muscle that keeps their metabolism, strength, and energy intact.

Most weight-loss tools count pounds. MemoryAisle counts body composition. It generates daily, protein-first meal plans and grocery lists, tracks lean mass over time, and adapts in real time to how you actually feel — quieter meals on the hard days after a shot, fuller portions when your appetite returns.

Why it matters

Research on GLP-1 weight loss has found that a substantial share of the weight people lose — by some measures around 39% — can be lean mass rather than fat. Lose muscle and you lose the very things that make weight loss worth it: a healthy metabolism, day-to-day strength, and long-term stability. MemoryAisle is designed to change that ratio, with protein targets and dose-cycle-aware guidance at the center of every day.

"When people around me started GLP-1s, the weight came off fast — and a lot of it was muscle. Every tool counted pounds; none of them cared what those pounds were made of. I built MemoryAisle so you can lose the fat and keep the strength that makes it worth doing." — Kevin Minn, Founder, MemoryAisle

About MemoryAisle

MemoryAisle is a body-composition-first nutrition app led by Mira, an AI companion you talk to by voice or text. Mira knows your protein target, training schedule, and where you are in your medication cycle, and turns that into one clear next meal at a time — with privacy built in: medication details stay on your device, voice is never recorded, and health data is encrypted twice. Built by SLTR Digital LLC in Los Angeles. Free to start; Pro is $49.99/year.
