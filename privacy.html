3. Categories of Data Collected

3.1 Data Stored Exclusively On-Device (Device-Local Data)

The following categories of data are stored exclusively on your device within the application's protected data container. This data is encrypted at rest by the device operating system using your device passcode. We do not have access to, cannot retrieve, and cannot view this data under any circumstances:

  • Body Composition Records: Weight, body fat percentage, lean body mass, progress photographs, and related measurements
  • Meal Records and Meal Plans: Individual meal entries, AI-generated meal plans, recipe data, and nutritional breakdowns
  • Training Session Records: Exercise and fitness activity logs
  • Gastrointestinal Tolerance Records: Food tolerance tracking data related to medication side effects
  • Provider Reports: Health summaries generated for sharing with your healthcare providers
  • Grocery Lists: Shopping list items, categories, and completion status
  • Pantry Inventory: Items in your household pantry as logged manually or via receipt scanner
  • Nutrition Logs: Daily caloric intake, macronutrient breakdowns, hydration tracking
  • Symptom Logs: Self-reported symptoms including but not limited to nausea, appetite changes, energy levels, bloating, constipation, and food aversions
  • Progress Photographs: Images captured or imported for body composition tracking, stored as encrypted binary data

Important notice regarding Device-Local Data: Because this data exists solely on your device, if your device is lost, stolen, destroyed, or reset without a prior backup, this data cannot be recovered by us or any third party. We strongly recommend maintaining regular device backups.

3.2 Authentication Credentials

Your authentication credentials, specifically your email address and session tokens, are stored in the device's hardware-backed secure credential storage. This storage provides encryption that is accessible only when the device is unlocked by the authenticated user. These credentials are never stored in plaintext or any unencrypted storage mechanism.

3.3 Health Platform Integration

With your explicit permission, MemoryAisle integrates with the device's native health platform to read and write the following categories of health data:

Data Read:

  • Body weight
  • Lean body mass
  • Body fat percentage

Data Written:

  • Dietary calories (from logged meals)
  • Dietary protein (from logged meals)

Critical disclosure: Health platform data is held in volatile memory (RAM) only for the duration of active app use. MemoryAisle does not persist, cache, write to disk, or transmit health platform data to any server, cloud service, or third party. When the App is closed or suspended, all health platform data in memory is immediately and irrevocably discarded.

3.4 Data Transmitted to Cloud Infrastructure (Opt-In Only)

Cloud synchronization is entirely optional and requires your express, affirmative action. No data is automatically transmitted to our servers. Upon initiating a sync, the following data is transmitted:

  • User Profile: Age, biological sex, current weight, goal weight, medication class (anonymized), dosage tier (anonymized), training level, dietary mode, and calculated nutritional targets
  • Nutrition Logs: Historical caloric and macronutrient intake records
  • Symptom Logs: Historical self-reported symptom data
  • Pantry Items: Current pantry inventory

Data NOT transmitted to cloud infrastructure under any circumstances:

  • Body composition records and progress photographs
  • Training session records
  • Meal plans and individual meal records
  • Gastrointestinal tolerance records
  • Grocery lists
  • Private journal entries
  • Provider reports

Cloud-synced data is transmitted over encrypted connections and stored in encrypted databases with managed encryption keys. Access to this data is restricted to authenticated API calls requiring valid user credentials.

3.5 Data Processed by Artificial Intelligence Services

When you interact with Mira, our AI nutrition companion, your typed or spoken message is transmitted to a secure AI processing service for response generation. To protect your privacy, we employ a purpose-built anonymization layer that processes your contextual data before transmission:

What IS sent to the AI service:

  • Your typed or spoken message text
  • Anonymized medication class (generic category rather than specific brand)
  • Anonymized dosage tier (categorical level rather than specific amount)
  • Derived nutritional metrics only: protein target, protein consumed today, water consumed, training level, dietary restrictions

What is NEVER sent to the AI service:

  • Your real name
  • Your email address
  • Your age or date of birth
  • Your biological sex
  • Your actual body weight or height
  • Your goal weight
  • Your progress photographs
  • Your journal entries
  • Your provider reports
  • Your location data
  • Any device identifiers

Important disclosure: While we anonymize contextual data, the content of your messages to Mira is transmitted as-is. If you voluntarily include personal information in your messages, that information will be processed by the AI service. We recommend exercising discretion in the information you share in chat messages.

We do not use your Mira conversations to train, improve, or fine-tune any AI models. Your conversations are not shared with, sold to, or disclosed to any third party.

3.6 Private Journal Data

The Private Journal feature is subject to enhanced privacy protections:

  • Journal entries are stored exclusively on your device
  • Journal entries are never transmitted to our servers, cloud infrastructure, AI services, or any third party under any circumstances
  • Access to the journal requires biometric authentication (Face ID or Touch ID), enforced at the application level before any content is rendered
  • We have no technical capability to access, read, recover, or reconstruct journal entries
  • Journal entries cannot be subpoenaed from us because we do not possess them

3.7 Data We Do NOT Collect

For the avoidance of doubt, MemoryAisle does NOT collect, process, store, or transmit:

  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device advertising identifiers
  • Browsing history or web activity
  • Contacts or address book data
  • Call logs or SMS messages
  • Financial information, payment card data, or banking information
  • Social media account information
  • Biometric data (biometric authentication data is processed solely by the device operating system)
  • Device sensor data beyond what is described herein
  • Cross-app tracking data
  • Data from other applications on your device

5. Data Anonymization and Minimization

5.1 Medication Anonymization

MemoryAisle employs a purpose-built anonymization engine that transforms sensitive medication information before any external transmission:

  • Brand names are replaced with generic pharmacological class labels
  • Specific dosage amounts are replaced with categorical tiers
  • This anonymization is performed on-device before data leaves the application boundary

5.2 Data Minimization Principles

We adhere to the principle of data minimization as follows:

  • We collect only the data necessary to provide the specific features you choose to use
  • Cloud synchronization transmits only the minimum data set required for backup and cross-device access
  • AI processing receives only anonymized, derived metrics rather than raw personal data
  • Health platform data is never persisted beyond volatile memory
  • No data is collected for purposes unrelated to the direct functionality of the App

6. Data Security Measures

6.1 Encryption

  • At Rest (Device): All Device-Local Data is encrypted by the device operating system. Application data stores are within a sandboxed container providing process-level isolation.
  • At Rest (Cloud): Cloud-synced data is encrypted using managed encryption keys with industry-standard encryption.
  • In Transit: All network communications are encrypted using current transport layer security protocols.
  • Credentials: Authentication credentials are stored in hardware-backed secure storage accessible only when the device is unlocked.

6.2 Access Controls

  • Biometric Authentication: The Private Journal and Provider Report features require biometric authentication before rendering any content.
  • Credential Access Control: Authentication tokens are accessible only when the device is unlocked by the authenticated user.
  • Server-Side Authorization: All API endpoints require valid authentication tokens. Infrastructure access is governed by least-privilege access policies.

6.3 Infrastructure Security

Our cloud infrastructure employs web application firewalls, audit logging of all API and infrastructure access, continuous threat detection and monitoring, and dedicated isolated environments. We conduct regular security reviews of our infrastructure and application code.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

7.1 Device-Local Data

Device-Local Data persists on your device until you: (a) manually delete it within the App; (b) delete the App from your device; or (c) erase or reset your device. We have no control over and no access to Device-Local Data retention.

7.2 Cloud-Synced Data

Cloud-synced data is retained for the duration of your account's existence. Upon account deletion (see Section 7.4), all cloud-synced data is permanently and irrevocably deleted from our systems.

7.3 AI Processing Data

Messages sent to Mira are processed in real-time and are not persistently stored by the AI processing service after the response is generated. We do not maintain a server-side log of your Mira conversations. Operational execution logs are retained for a maximum of seven (7) days for debugging purposes and are automatically purged thereafter.

7.4 Account Deletion

You may delete your account and all associated cloud-synced data at any time through the Profile section of the App. Upon initiating account deletion:

  • A confirmation dialog will be presented to prevent accidental deletion
  • All cloud-synced data will be permanently deleted from our servers
  • All local data stores will be wiped from the device
  • Your authentication session will be terminated
  • Your user record will be deactivated

Account deletion is irreversible. Once completed, we cannot recover any data associated with your account.

7.5 Data Portability

You may export your data at any time through the App's built-in export functionality. We support data portability rights as required under applicable state privacy laws.
