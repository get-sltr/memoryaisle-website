Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

The short version: Your medication details never leave your phone. Your voice is never recorded. Your body data stays in Apple Health. We never sell your data to anyone. You can delete everything at any time.

Who we are

MemoryAisle is operated by SLTR Digital LLC, a company registered in Los Angeles, California. When we say "we," "us," or "our," we mean SLTR Digital LLC.

What data we collect

We classify all data into tiers based on sensitivity. The tier determines how it is stored, encrypted, and accessed.

Critical data (stays on your device)

  • GLP-1 medication type and dosage
  • Injection or dose schedule and timestamps
  • Symptom logs (nausea, appetite, side effects)
  • Body weight and body composition readings
  • Provider report exports

This data is stored in your device's local database, encrypted by iOS Data Protection. When synced to our servers, it is encrypted with AES-256-GCM before leaving your device. Our AI assistant never receives this data in identifiable form.

Sensitive data (encrypted on our servers)

  • Meal plans and nutrition logs
  • Grocery lists and pantry inventory
  • Barcode scan history
  • Mira conversation history
  • User profile (name, email, goals, dietary restrictions)

This data is encrypted at rest using AWS server-side encryption (SSE-KMS) and in transit using TLS 1.3.

Data we do NOT collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Browsing history
  • Financial information (payments handled by Apple)
  • Biometric data (Face ID/Touch ID stays in Apple's Secure Enclave)

How we use your data

We use your data solely to provide the MemoryAisle service:

  • Generate personalized meal plans based on your nutrition targets
  • Power Mira's AI recommendations (using anonymized context only)
  • Track your nutrition progress over time
  • Generate provider reports at your request

What Mira sees

When you talk to Mira, your medication details are anonymized on your device before anything is sent. Mira receives:

  • Medication class (e.g., "GLP-1 agonist") - not brand names
  • Dose tier (e.g., "low") - not exact dosages
  • Days since dose - not calendar dates
  • Symptom state (e.g., "mild nausea") - not detailed logs
  • Protein and calorie targets and current intake

Mira never receives your name, email, body weight, or any information that could identify you personally.

Your rights

Delete your data: Settings > Privacy > Delete My Account. All server data is permanently erased within 30 days.

Export your data: Settings > Privacy > Export My Data. Receive all your data as a JSON file.

California residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what data we collect, request deletion, and opt out of data sales. We do not sell your data.

Children's privacy

MemoryAisle is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

We may update this policy as our practices evolve. We will notify you of material changes through the app or email.
