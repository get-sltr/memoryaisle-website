Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

Welcome to MemoryAisle. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how SLTR Digital LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects your personal information when you access or use the MemoryAisle mobile application ("App") and related services (collectively, the "Services"). This Privacy Policy should be read together with our Terms of Use.

By creating an account, accessing, or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described herein. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following categories to provide, maintain, and improve the Services:

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: When you register for an account, we collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials. If you choose to sign in using a third-party authentication provider (such as Apple Sign In or Google Sign In), we receive limited profile information as permitted by your settings with that provider
  • Family and Household Data: Names, dietary preferences, dietary restrictions, food allergies, intolerances, and other health-related information you provide for yourself and your family members
  • User Content: Grocery lists, shopping items, recipes, meal plans, family traditions, notes, store preferences, and any other content you create, upload, or store within the App
  • Communication Data: Information you provide when you contact us for support, submit feedback, or communicate with us through any channel

1.2 Information Collected Through App Features

  • Mira AI Interactions: Queries, prompts, and conversations you have with the Mira AI assistant, along with your family preference data used to generate personalized responses
  • Receipt Data: When you use the receipt scanning feature, we process photographs of store receipts to extract item names, prices, and other transaction data using optical character recognition (OCR) technology
  • Voice Data: When you use voice command features, audio data is processed in real time to convert speech to text. Audio recordings are not permanently stored on our servers
  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect precise location data to provide store geofencing features, including store arrival and departure notifications

1.3 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and general device settings
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the App, including features used, actions taken, frequency and duration of use, and interaction patterns
  • Performance Data: Crash reports, error logs, and performance metrics to help us identify and resolve technical issues

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide and Maintain Services: To operate the App, manage your account, deliver features you request, and provide customer support
  • Personalization: To customize your experience, including personalized grocery suggestions, meal plans, recipe recommendations, and AI-powered assistance through Mira
  • Location-Based Features: To send store arrival and departure notifications through geofencing, enabling relevant shopping list reminders when you are near a designated store
  • Receipt Processing: To extract and organize item and price data from scanned receipts for your convenience
  • Family Sharing: To facilitate sharing of lists, meal plans, and other content among members of your family group
  • Communications: To send you service-related notifications, account updates, security alerts, and respond to your inquiries
  • Improvement and Development: To analyze usage patterns, diagnose technical issues, and develop new features and improvements to the Services
  • Safety and Security: To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, security risks, and technical issues
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, and enforceable governmental requests

3. Mira AI Assistant and Data Processing

Mira is an AI-powered assistant integrated into the App. Understanding how Mira handles your data is important to us:

3.1 What Data Mira Processes

  • Your conversation messages and queries
  • Family dietary preferences, allergies, and restrictions you have stored in the App
  • Shopping history and patterns relevant to generating personalized suggestions

3.2 How Mira Data Is Handled

  • Mira queries are transmitted to third-party AI service providers for the purpose of generating responses. These providers process your data solely to fulfill your request and are contractually prohibited from using your data to train their models or for any other purpose
  • Mira does not access external accounts, contact lists, or data sources beyond what you explicitly provide within the App
  • Conversation history with Mira is stored in your account to provide context for future interactions

Your Control Over Mira Data

You can clear your entire Mira conversation history at any time through the App settings. Deleted conversations are permanently removed from our active systems.

For Mira-specific questions or concerns, contact: mira@memoryaisle.app

4. Location Data

MemoryAisle uses location data to power store geofencing features. We are committed to handling your location data responsibly:

  • Location access requires your explicit permission through your device's operating system. You may grant or revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings
  • Location data is used solely for the purpose of providing store-proximity notifications
  • Location data is not tracked, stored, or retained on our servers beyond the active session
  • We do not share your location data with any third party for advertising, marketing, analytics, or any purpose unrelated to providing the Services
  • Background location access ("Always") is used only for geofencing notifications and can be disabled at any time without affecting other App features

5. Receipt Scanning and Image Data

  • Receipt images are processed using OCR technology to extract item and pricing information
  • Receipt images may contain sensitive information such as partial payment card numbers or store loyalty identifiers. You are solely responsible for the content of images you scan
  • Extracted receipt data is stored securely in your account
  • We do not use receipt data to build advertising profiles or share it with third-party marketers

6. Voice Command Data

  • Audio data from voice commands is processed in real time to convert speech to text
  • Audio recordings are not permanently stored on our servers. Audio data is discarded immediately after the command is processed
  • Voice features require microphone permission, which you can revoke at any time through your device settings

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

WE DO NOT SELL, RENT, TRADE, OR OTHERWISE DISTRIBUTE YOUR PERSONAL DATA TO ANY THIRD PARTY FOR MARKETING, ADVERTISING, OR DATA BROKERING PURPOSES. WE NEVER HAVE AND NEVER WILL.

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

7.1 Service Providers

We engage trusted third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf. These providers are contractually obligated to use your data only for the specific services they provide to us and are required to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information. Our service providers fall into the following categories:

  • Cloud Infrastructure and Database Providers: For secure data storage, authentication, and real-time synchronization of your account data across devices
  • AI Service Providers: For processing Mira AI queries and generating responses. Your interaction data is transmitted solely for the purpose of fulfilling your request and is not used to train third-party models
  • Payment Processors: All subscription payments are processed exclusively through Apple's App Store. We do not directly collect, store, or process any payment card information

7.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request, or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to: (a) comply with applicable law; (b) protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or the public; (c) detect, prevent, or address fraud, security, or technical issues; or (d) enforce our Terms of Use.

7.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, bankruptcy, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your personal information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email and/or prominent notice in the App of any change in ownership or use of your personal information.

7.4 Family Group Members

Content you share within a family group (lists, meal plans, recipes, etc.) is visible to all members of that group. Family member profiles, including dietary preferences and allergy information, may be visible to other group members to facilitate shared planning.

8. Data Retention

Minimal Data Retention

WE DO NOT RETAIN DATA BEYOND WHAT IS NECESSARY TO PROVIDE THE SERVICES. YOUR DATA EXISTS TO SERVE YOU, NOT US.

  • Active Accounts: Your data is retained for as long as your account remains active and as needed to provide you with the Services
  • Account Deletion: When you delete your account, all personal data, User Content, family profiles, lists, meal plans, and Mira conversation history will be permanently deleted from our active systems within thirty (30) days and from backup systems within ninety (90) days
  • Legal Requirements: Certain data may be retained beyond these periods as required by applicable law, regulation, legal proceedings, or legitimate business purposes such as fraud prevention. Any retained data will be stored securely and used solely for the purpose for which it was retained
  • Location Data: Not retained beyond the active session
  • Voice Data: Not retained after command processing

9. Data Security

We implement and maintain appropriate technical, administrative, and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Secure authentication mechanisms and access controls
  • Regular security assessments and monitoring
  • Strict access controls limiting employee and contractor access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Row-level security policies ensuring users can only access their own data

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to maintaining industry-standard protections and promptly addressing any security incidents.

10. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You may access and view your personal data through the App at any time
  • Correction: You may update or correct your personal information through your account settings
  • Deletion: You may delete your account and all associated data through the App settings or by contacting us at delete@memoryaisle.app
  • Data Export: You may request a copy of your data in a commonly used, machine-readable format by contacting support@memoryaisle.app
  • Withdraw Consent: You may withdraw consent for optional data collection (such as location services, camera access, and microphone access) at any time through your device settings
  • Opt-Out of Communications: You may opt out of non-essential communications at any time. Service-related notifications necessary for the operation of your account may not be opted out of while your account is active
  • Clear AI History: You may clear your Mira conversation history at any time through the App settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@memoryaisle.app. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with applicable law.

11. Children's Privacy

MemoryAisle is designed for families, but accounts must be created by individuals who are at least thirteen (13) years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child under 13 has provided personal information to us or created an account, please contact us immediately at legal@memoryaisle.app. We will promptly investigate and delete the child's account and all associated data.

Family profiles may include information about children (such as dietary preferences and allergies) as entered by a parent or guardian. This information is stored within the parent or guardian's account and is under their control.

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Right to Know: You have the right to request that we disclose what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell about you
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions
  • Right to Correct: You have the right to request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We do not sell your personal information. We have never sold personal information and have no plans to do so
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your privacy rights
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information: You have the right to limit the use and disclosure of sensitive personal information to what is necessary for providing the Services

To exercise your California privacy rights, contact us at privacy@memoryaisle.app or legal@memoryaisle.app. We will verify your identity before processing your request and respond within forty-five (45) days as required by law.

13. International Users

The App is operated from the United States. If you are accessing the App from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States, where our servers are located and our central database is operated. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States.

We are committed to complying with applicable data protection laws in the jurisdictions where we operate. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or other regions with data protection laws, you may have additional rights regarding your personal data. Contact us at privacy@memoryaisle.app for more information.

14. Device Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions to provide specific features. All permissions are optional and can be managed through your device settings:

  • Camera: Required for the receipt scanning feature. Not used for any other purpose
  • Microphone: Required for voice command features. Audio is processed in real time and not stored
  • Location (When In Use / Always): Required for store geofencing notifications. "Always" permission enables background arrival/departure notifications. Location data is not stored beyond the active session
  • Notifications: Used to deliver reminders, list updates, family sharing alerts, and store proximity notifications

Denying any permission will disable the specific feature that requires it but will not affect the core functionality of the App.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any third-party websites or services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites or services you visit.

16. Do Not Track Signals
