II · Frequently Asked

Answers to the most common questions.

Is MemoryAisle a medical app?

No. MemoryAisle is a nutrition planning tool. It does not diagnose conditions, recommend medication changes, or provide medical advice. Always consult your prescriber for medical questions.

Does MemoryAisle see my prescription?

No. Your medication details stay on your phone. Our AI only receives anonymous categories like "GLP-1, low dose, day 2." We never see brand names or exact dosages.

Can I use MemoryAisle without a GLP-1 medication?

Yes. During onboarding, select "No, I just want smarter nutrition." The app works for anyone focused on protein-first eating and body composition.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to iPhone Settings › Your Name › Subscriptions › MemoryAisle › Cancel.

What medications are supported?

Ozempic, Wegovy, Wegovy Pill, Mounjaro, Zepbound, Foundayo, Rybelsus, and compounded semaglutide/tirzepatide.
